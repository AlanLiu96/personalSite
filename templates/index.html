Projects

Anon - anonmarket.com

Yale startup

An online marketplace for digital files, allowing users to upload their own creative content and sell it anonymously.It utilizes a django based backend to provide the infrastructure and files for other users to download. Now, it targets an international audience as any user from China to Germany can download and upload files as well.

Reservations - Github

Worked with Yale Reservations Team

Reservations is an equipment tracking system for Yale's media libraries. It lets you reserve and checkout media equipment like cameras and tripods just like you would with books. The organized database makes handling complicated processes like late fees and reminder emails much simpler.

iDuino - DevPost

HackPrinceton Spring 2015 - Overall Hardware 2nd Place

iDuino brings accessibility and ease of use of other child-friendly visual programming languages like Scratch to hardware programming. It utilizes Bluetooth to transmit 1 byte commands in our original designed "coding language" from our iPhone app to the arduino allowing the user to control which ports to use and their functions.

Habitar - habitar.me

YHack 2014 - Best Use of MongoDB API

Habitars are little fitness buddies that grow and evolve as you complete your habits daily. They are designed to motivate the user to follow through on their habits. It uses a MongoDB backend to keep track of each of our users' tasks and goals.
